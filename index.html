Welcome to Homebased.

Come home in style! Homebased performs tasks when you arrive or leave home. It uses your location and connects to the Internet of Things to accomplish its goal.

This Windows Phone 8.1 app was created as a result of a New Year's resolution to procrastinate less and spend some spare time towards producing something, anything!
